8. Most Important Advice
Resist the urge to build everything at once. Follow the sprint plan strictly. Weeks 1-2 are
ONLY about project scaffold and CI/CD. That's it.
The single biggest risk to this project is scope creep in the first month. The documents are
comprehensive and tempt you to build everything immediately. Don't. Build the scaffold first, then the
agent core, then routing. One sprint at a time.
